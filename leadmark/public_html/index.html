About Company

Thinktantra is a website in which you can publish your articles, research papers and notes. This is such an online platform in which a student, teacher, professors or working professional can share his own thinking and his own experiences. Here professors and working professionals can provide notes of subjects in which they have expertise. Common people can also write their articles here, in which they will be able to publish their articles and they can hide their identity also.

Many times it happens that when a student goes for the notes of a subject on the Internet, then he does not find them according to his needs or he does not understand the notes, by solving this problem, Thinktantra's website will give variety to the students in which They will be able to find simple and verified teachers' notes. Through this platform, our aim is that everyone can publish their research papers also.
